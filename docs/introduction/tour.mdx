Welcome to the Grand Tour! You may have already gone through the quickstart and run some sample code. Now it's time to see what you can build with this framework.

This guide shows how to build AI applications, from your first agent to production-ready systems, with sections you can follow step-by-step or skip to what interests you.

## Journey Overview

Here's a quick look at the stages you'll go through and the modules you'll use:

| Stage | What You'll Build | Key Modules |
|-------|------------------|-------------|
| **[Foundation](#foundation)** | Your first simple chat agent | [Agent](https://framework.beeai.dev/modules/agents), [Backend](https://framework.beeai.dev/modules/backend) |
| **[Intelligence](#intelligence)** | An agent that can remember context and take actions | [Memory](https://framework.beeai.dev/modules/memory), [Tools](https://framework.beeai.dev/modules/tools), [Templates](https://framework.beeai.dev/modules/templates) |
| **[Knowledge](#knowledge)** | An agent grounded in your documents and data | [RAG](https://framework.beeai.dev/modules/rag) |
| **[Orchestration](#orchestration)** | A team of specialized agents working together | [Workflows](https://framework.beeai.dev/modules/workflows) |
| **[Production](#production)** | Enterprise-ready infrastructure for reliability and scale | [Cache](https://framework.beeai.dev/modules/cache), [Observability](https://framework.beeai.dev/modules/observability), [Serialization](https://framework.beeai.dev/modules/serialization), [Errors](https://framework.beeai.dev/modules/errors) |
| **[Integration](#integration)** | Make your agent accessible as a service (MCP, A2A) | [Serve](https://framework.beeai.dev/modules/serve) |
